Fun Week!

Read Post
Visit Us

This week we will be celebrating the books we love for world book day. We invite children and staff to come dressed as their favourite character.

In our preschool room we are going to be making our own stories, in the mark making have a super worn tuff tray and tiger who comes for tea in our role play area. We are looking forward to our children bringing in their favourite books so we can read plenty of stories.

Toddlers have a ” going on a bear hunt” theme in place and are making binoculars to hunt for a bear that’s hiding in the room. They will have a teddy bears picnic too.

Babies will be having lots of play with small character’s. They will be decorating biscuits and listening to stories.

We can’t wait to see you all next week.
